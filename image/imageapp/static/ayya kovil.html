The temple is known as Ayya Vaikunda Dharmapathi Kovil (also called Ayya Kovil / Ayya Kovil) in Manali Pudhugaar / Manali New Town, Chennai. It is a very popular temple among devotees, especially during certain religious occasions. One of the major festivals is the Rajagopur annual festival (“ரაჯகோபுர ஆண்டு திருவிழா”), which sees large participation. Maalaimalar Another important observance is Ayya Vaikunda Avathara Thirunal (Ayya Vaikundar’s incarnation day), which is celebrated with a procession.
